--- layout: post title: "BTCPing.me: Simple, free, Bitcoin price alerts" date: 2014-06-14 ---

I've been using a popular Bitcoin price notification service to get notified when the price of Bitcoin drops below certain thresholds on certain exchanges. Last week I ran out of free account credits. I thought I could build something that covered the base functionality I needed out of the service.

I now present to you BTCPing.me!

It's a very minimal app that does the same sort of threshold-based email price alerts as these other services. I have a huge account credit with DigitalOcean that I got as a bounty for a "responsible disclosure" so I am running this for free as a public service on a small VPS there. If you want to host your own instead you can grab source at https://github.com/nzaillian/btcping.me.
